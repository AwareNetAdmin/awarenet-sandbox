Feature Summary

What AwareNet brings to your neighborhood.

Consolidation and federation of automated alerts sent by otherwise standalone security cameras.
Capacity to support hundreds of cameras working together to help keep your neighborhood safe.
Compatible with dozens of security cameras costing less than $200 each, available on Amazon or directly from the camera manufacturer.
Each camera owner maintains control of the privacy and security features for their camera.
Easy-to-use software application for review and categorization of real-time alerts. Securely accessible via the internet and ideal for use on a tablet.
Designated reviewer (e.g. security guard or officer) can assess real-time alerts and take immediate action when appropriate to disrupt and deter criminal activity.
Important time stamps to help ensure the reviewer is processing alerts in a timely manner.
GPS mapping to help ensure the patrol person is covering the full service area.
False alerts are minimized with camera AI that identifies motion as activity by a person, a vehicle, or something else.
Ability to disable alerts for a camera when it is sending too many alerts (e.g. block party, configuration issue, etc.).
Map of all camera locations with status information for each camera and ability to re-enable alerts.
Easy export of data for analysis and reporting using any spreadsheet app.
