Review Alert

How the designated reviewer monitors and acts on alerts.

Alert List

The main page for the reviewer is a list of alerts.

  • The list automatically updates with the newest alerts at the top of the list.
  • The reviewer can filter the list to suit their needs (e.g. Time/Date, Street Name, Event, Category).

Reviewing an Alert

Selecting an alert on the main page displays the review page for that alert.

  • The address is a link to display Google Maps for that address.
  • The compass direction in parenthesis is the direction the camera is facing.
  • The reviewer categorizes each alert they review (i.e. False, Unremarkable, Unusual, Suspicious, Criminal).
  • A comment is required for Unusual, Suspicious, and Criminal categories.
  • The purple and blue buttons are quick navigation to other alerts (Previous On Block, Next On Block, Previous In Neighborhood, Next In Neighborhood).
